Who are we?

Omny IQ was created to eliminate the frustration associated with poor connected experiences.  Our cofounders have built and run large connected device businesses and share a common vision about the pain point that poor User experience kills market adoption. Too often device companies have the old "ship and forget" mentality.  They won't make it in the world of the Internet of Things, where the market will explode only if it can attract the biggest segment of the population: the less technical User.
​Users will dump devices that frustrate them and
they deserve products that provide easy, reliable operation, like a toaster.

Team

The cofounders of Omny IQ have decades of experience leading companies that deliver some of the most popular connected devices.
